Zadanie 31

Oblicz granicę lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{n \sin(n+3)}{(3n-1)^2}.

1️⃣ Korzystamy z twierdzenia o trzech ciągach.

-1 \leq \sin(n+3) \leq 1

c_n = \frac{n}{(3n-1)^2} > 0 \quad a_n = \frac{-n}{(3n-1)^2} < 0 \quad b_n = \frac{n \sin(n+3)}{(3n-1)^2}

\frac{-n}{(3n-1)^2} \leq \frac{n \sin(n+3)}{(3n-1)^2} \leq \frac{n}{(3n-1)^2}

Zatem \lim_{n \to \infty} b_n = 0

Odp.: \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{n \sin(n+3)}{(3n-1)^2} = 0.
