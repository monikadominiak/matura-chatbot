Zadanie 32

Oblicz granicę lim n→∞ cos(n^2+n)/(5n+1). 

1️⃣ Korzystamy z twierdzenia o trzech ciągach.
\[
-1 \leq \cos(n^2+n) \leq 1
\]
\[
-\frac{1}{5n+1} \leq \frac{\cos(n^2+n)}{5n+1} \leq \frac{1}{5n+1}
\]
\[
\lim_{n \to \infty} -\frac{1}{5n+1} = 0
\]
\[
\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{5n+1} = 0
\]
Zatem \(\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{\cos(n^2+n)}{5n+1} = 0\)

Odp.: \(\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{\cos(n^2+n)}{5n+1} = 0.\)
